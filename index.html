Members of the Month

  • April 2023

    Nick Norman

    Congratulations to Nick Norman for being selected as the April Member of the Month! Nick is a freshman in NPRE from St. Louis, MO and is interested in the plasma concentration because of the potential of fusion energy. In April, Nick volunteered at EOH, attended the ANS Student Conference, and was elected as secretary for next year!

  • March 2023

    Vincent Cauilan

    Congratulations to Vincent Cauilan for being selected as the March Member of the Month! Vincent is a junior in NPRE from Carol Stream, IL and is interested in the plasma concentration because it is a niche yet useful technology that holds great promise. In March, Vincent attended a general meeting, volunteered at EOH, and was instrumental in getting the UCRT model reactor's electronics running.

  • February 2023

    Rhys MacMillan

    Congratulations to Rhys MacMillan for being selected as the February Member of the Month! Rhys is a freshman in NPRE from Nashville, TN and is interested in the power concentration because of the pressing need for low carbon energy sources. In February, Rhys attended a general meeting, outreach meeting, and two underclassmen round table meetings where he worked on the cloud chamber and the presentation of the model reactor.

  • January 2023

    Logan Blume

    Logan is a freshman in NPRE from Chicago, IL (Go Cubs!) and is in the plasma and fusion concentration. Logan believes fusion energy has the potential to change the world and can help us achieve net zero emissions. In January, Logan attended the general meeting and both underclassmen round table meetings, where he led the development of the model nuclear reactor.

  • November 2022

    Case Biros

    Case is a freshman in NPRE from Mazon, IL and is in the power concentration. In November, Case attended the general meeting, an outreach meeting, the board game night, and went to happy hour.

  • October 2022

    Piper Fernau

    Piper is a freshman in NPRE from York, Nebraska and is in the plasma concentration. In October, Piper attended both general meetings, poster making, helped at the campus sustainability celebration, volunteered three hours at the green quad day booth, and volunteered four hours at the NSW booth!

  • September 2022

    Drew Gura

    Drew is a freshman in NPRE from Romeoville Illinois and plans on pursuing the radiological concentration while potentially minoring in bioengineering. In September, Drew attended both general meetings, the outreach meeting, and volunteered at iHelp!
